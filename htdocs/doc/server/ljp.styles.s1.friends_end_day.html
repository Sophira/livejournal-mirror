FRIENDS_END_DAY

FRIENDS_END_DAY — The opposite of FRIENDS_NEW_DAY. This variable is inserted into %%events%% in the FRIENDS_EVENTS.

View Types:

friends

Overrideable:

No; users of the style cannot override this. It may only be defined in the style.
