3. Where to go from here

S2 Manual
We've produced a manual covering every topic related to using S2 on LiveJournal. As a matter of fact you're currently reading a chapter from the manual. To start from the beginning, navigate to S2 Manual.
LJ Style Discussion
LJ Style Discussion is a community on LiveJournal.com that covers most S1 and S2 discussions. This community has been sanctioned by administrators of LiveJournal as an “official” community.
S2 Practice Discussion
S2 Practice Discussion is a community on LiveJournal.com that deals with introducing S2 to members of LiveJournal's support team. Although geared toward support volunteers, participation is open to all.
